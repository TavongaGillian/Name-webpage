ORIGIN

The name Tavonga is a shona baby name that can be given to both baby girls and baby boys. Shona Language is the main language spoken in Zimbabwe and thats where the name originated from. Names with the same meaning as Tavonga also exist in other languages for example in zulu there is Siyabonga, Merci in french and Jayden in Hebrew. Tavonga means thank you and most people who name theirs babbies Tavonga are shona parents giving thanks to God.
